Description / Arcade Record Cabinet

The Arcade Record Cabinet pays homage to the golden age of vinyl with storage designed for the modern audiophile. Generous compartments sized for twelve-inch records slope gently forward, making it effortless to flip through your collection. The softly rounded corners and edges evoke vintage consoles while remaining unmistakably contemporary.

Crafted from oiled oak with a honey-warm tone, the cabinet features a recessed top shelf perfect for your turntable setup, with a discreet cable port at the rear. Lower open compartments provide additional storage for amplifiers, books, or decorative objects. The piece stands on cylindrical brass legs that elevate it just enough for easy cleaning beneath.

Dimensions: 120 × 45 × 75 cm (W × D × H)

Materials: Solid and veneered oiled oak, brushed brass cylindrical legs, integrated cable management
