COMMON QUESTIONS

HOW BIG IS A STANDARD BAREFOOT BUTTON?

The Standard is our most popular button size. It measures 1 1/8" in diameter and 1/2" in height.

THE SIZE STACK UP
WILL THIS FIT MY SWITCH?

The V1 Standard fits 3PDT footswitches with a 3/8" (10mm) switch head diameter. Before ordering, measure your switch  to confirm. Check out our Fit Guide for more info.

  FIT GUIDE→

HOW DO I INSTALL BAREFOOT BUTTONS?

1. PREPARE

Use the Allen wrench and set screws included with every button.

2. INSTALL

Place the button on your swtich.

 

3. SECURE

Use the Allen wrench to tighten the set screws snugly to the switch.

4. PLAY

That’s it. Stomp away!

 

WILL THIS DAMAGE MY SWTICH OR FALL OFF?

No. Every Barefoot Button comes with 3 set screws and an allen wrench to secure the screws and set the button firmly in place. The screws snug up to your swtich head, but do not damage it. The button stays put without damaging the switch. Every buttons is precision machined to tight tolerances — it won't wobble, shift, or scratch your switch. 

CAN I MOVE IT BETWEEN PEDALS?

Yes, as long as the pedal you are moving to also has a 3PDT footswitch with a 3/8" (10mm) switch head. Using the allen wrench that comes with each Barefoot Button, loosen the set screws, lift it off, and reinstall on the new switch. Barefoot Buttons are precision machined to last — built to move with you from pedal to pedal, build to build, gig to gig.
